Train a new wakeword

Generates ~1100 synthetic samples in 6 voices using Piper TTS, augments with reverb/noise/pitch jitter, optionally mixes in pitch-shifted recordings of your voice, extracts features with OpenWakeWord, fits a logistic regression classifier, and saves a .joblib model. ~3 minutes on a Jetson Orin Nano.

🎙 Record yourself (optional but recommended)

The TTS voices are studio-clean; your room and mic aren't. Recording 10 short clips of yourself saying the keyword tells the classifier what your actual voice + room + mic sound like, and the trainer pitch-shifts those clips ±2 / ±4 semitones to also cover other people's voices. Typically lifts your confidence score from ~0.45 to ~0.90.
